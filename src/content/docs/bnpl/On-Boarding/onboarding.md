## Customer Onboarding Process

This document outlines the steps involved in onboarding a new customer.

1.  **Identity Verification**: The customer's identity is verified using a third-party service.
2.  **Credit Check**: A credit check is performed to determine the customer's creditworthiness.
3.  **Account Creation**: An account is created for the customer in the BNPL system.
4.  **Welcome Email**: The customer receives a welcome email with instructions on how to use the service.
